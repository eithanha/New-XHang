Games

A collection of games made in p5.js

Asteroids

A classic game of astroid. Press the space bar to shoot and hear the laser break the astroid into small pieces.


Flappy Bird

A recreation of the sensational game Flappy Bird. Press the space bar to make the bird jump between the pipes.


Pong

A recreation of Pong where you play against yourself. Right player is "W" and "S", left player is up and down arrow keys.
